Character Builder

This is intended for quick use for a person's first character. The choices are limited to those in the free basic D&D 5th edition rules.

As of now this is not a complete replacement of the rules, but more of a tool to help visualize your choices, and automatically fill calculations. For best results use this app with the quick builder in the rules.
